About Us


We, the students of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology Pune from Department of Multidisciplary Engineering, have created this personalized IOT based healthcare monitoring system. Created under the guidance of Prof. Mukund Kulkarni, our project aims to improve the field of personal healthcare and make it simpler and accessible.
The users of this project should be able to check their health parameters easily through visualization and dashboard.

We hope that you find it useful!
